About us

Our team covers the entire creative process: from game design, through 3D visuals to original sound and code. We all study digital game development at Skyro Modern High School based in Bratislava.

Our successes and projects.

TatraBanka Game design Gamejam Tabi "First Place"

The goal of the game jam was to design a game for the children's app Tabi, which teaches kids financial literacy (From the largest bank in Slovakia).
We decided to create something in the style of the board game Monopoly, but with our own original twist.

The player had a limited number of turns and their goal was to save up for a product they had chosen.
During the game, they had 3 to 5 rolls with two dice (depending on the selected difficulty level) and could land on three different types of tiles:

Question – The player received a question related to finance. For a correct answer, they earned Tatra Coins.

Bonus – The player had to make a financial decision, such as investing half of their Tatra Coins with a 50% chance to double them.

Penalty – The player lost a certain amount of Tatra Coins.

Each tile gave the player a card describing the situation that had occurred.

We were overwhelmed with joy and emotion when we found out that we were the winners.

Reclaim The Skyies "currently working on"

Our game is a fast-paced FPS inspired by Far Cry, set in a world of floating islands.
In the demo, your mission is to take back the Air Docks — a strategic skyport — and turn the tide in favor of the resistance.
Rescue the innocent. Eliminate the guilty.

You can go in guns blazing and level everything in your path, or use the fully functional stealth system to complete your objectives silently — earning special bonuses for staying undetected.

The core focus of the game is its advanced AI system, designed to react dynamically to the player’s actions, whether you’re sneaking in the shadows or leading a full-frontal assault.

Bubbly "Gamejam"

Our first team project is a top-down roguelike game.
You play as a bunny named Bubbly, and your goal is to catch all the foxes in bubbles so they can collect carrots for you.
Over time, you’ll be able to afford various upgrades, such as a Bubble Shotgun.

The game was created during a 48-hour game jam with the theme: Bubble.
It is free and playable on Itch.io.
